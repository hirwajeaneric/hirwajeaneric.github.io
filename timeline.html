In learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it is very important to also care about practicing and doing tasks and also projects. This will help us to assess our improvement, understanding, and without forgetting the gathering of different projects which we shall have completed up to the end of the challenge.

Therefore, bellow is a small structure of how the week of work is structure for this remaining six months of learning.

Main things to focus on in a week course.

  1. Revision of course content and expectations.
  2. Identification of samples that need to be followed in the course of learning and the project.
  3. Studing the content.
  4. Experimentation and practicing.
  5. Researching and using of other sources.
    Note:References have to be well recorded.
  6. Preparation of the project objectives and nature.
  7. Project.
  8. Project submittion.
  9. Project and result analysis for future improvements and projects.
