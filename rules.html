Rules

6 months web development challenge.

1.objectives.

We have set this challenge with the objective of inducing the spirit and desire of learning webdesign.

For students and other people who want to learn web development and design from scratch but also with freedom and time to recap and understand what they learn, we expect good results from this challenge as by the end of six months, one will have gathered enormous information about exclusively the basis of the front end part of any website.

The challenge focuses on:

  1. Simplicity where there is extra facilitation of the way the lessons will be acquired in comparision to other sources.

  2. Confort and flexibility of the course and tasks that we shall be doing.

  3. Giving the chance and guidance to all these who want to learn web development on their own.

  4. Giving learners a logical and most of all free time where the necessary information needed for everyone will be gathered despite the speed or how long one has taken learning.

  5. Training learners to set objectives on which to roll on in the course of the challenge where by one will end the challenge with a big number of finished and hosted project he/she is proud of.

  6. Gathering all the important information and resources that one can and will be using in and after the challenge.

2. Learning & submittion procedures.

  1. One must have a github account where he/she shall create the Challengerepository where he/she will be commiting his codes and works all along the challenge.

  2. One should also host on github an introductory webpage with a menu bar which shall be improved on the course of the challenge. This page will be the home page for all the other web pages one will post as projects during the challenge.

  3. Before the starting of the week module, we shall be reviewing the course contents and therefore, set objectives and expectations of what has to be acquired at the end.

  4. One shall be finding a reference or else sample work (project) that he or she will be doing at the end of the module.

  5. For all the 1 week long course challenges, there shall be a ending project at the end of the week module(s).

  6. The end module project is a combination or summary of elemets that one will be having acquired for that specific period.

  7. As a way to connect the dots of all things we shall learn, we shall be connecting what we have learnt previously with the new project objectives, correcting errors and things which did not go well and therefore not only improving in the current module, but also in the previous ones.

  8. As highligthed in the challenge timeline, there shall be free weeks or even low work weeks where we shall be implementing projects which specificaly circle all the previous content.

  9. Challenges will also be set as a measure to ensure constant learning and improvement.

  10. The submittion process will be structured as follow.

    • The for every submitted project, one shall be attaching in his webpage a reference block which we be containing aurl(s) of where he or she found the sample project of website he used during the end module project.

    • Copying codes is not allowed unless one is confident enought to explain them and manipulate them accordingly.

    • In cases where extra information was used in the course of finding more information, the urls will also be submitted.
